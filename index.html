Balint Kiraly, PhD
Postdoctoral Researcher in Cognitive Neuroscience at the Medical University of Vienna
Balint Kiraly, PhD

About me I am a neuroscientist fascinated by how the brain adapts to ever-changing environments. I focus on neuromodulatory processes that support this remarkable flexibility. I combine tools from artificial intelligence and physics to model and analyze neural dynamics. My goal is to uncover the principles of learning and plasticity in the healthy brain—and how these processes are disrupted in neurodegenerative diseases. Currently, I work as a postdoctoral researcher at the Medical University of Vienna, in the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Decision Support.

Key areas of focus:

  • Neural adaptation and plasticity
  • Neuromodulation and network-level changes
  • Computational modeling using AI tools
  • Bridging theory and experiment in brain research
