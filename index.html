Balint Kiraly, PhD
Postdoctoral Researcher in Cognitive Neuroscience at the Medical University of Vienna
About me

I am a neuroscientist fascinated by how the brain adapts to ever-changing environments. I focus on neuromodulatory processes that support this remarkable flexibility. I combine tools from artificial intelligence and physics to model and analyze neural dynamics. My goal is to uncover the principles of learning and plasticity in the healthy brain— and how these processes are disrupted in neurodegenerative diseases.
