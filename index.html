Balint Kiraly, PhD
Postdoctoral Researcher in Cognitive Neuroscience

I am a neuroscientist interested in how the brain adapts to changing environmental conditions. My primary focus is on neuromodulatory processes that can enable the brain's remarkable capacity for dynamic adaptation.

To investigate these mechanisms, I integrate methods from artificial intelligence and physics to analyze neural data and build models of underlying network dynamics.

By combining experimental neuroscience with computational and theoretical approaches, I aim to uncover the principles of learning and plasticity in the healthy brain—and how these processes are disrupted in neurological disorders such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's disease.
